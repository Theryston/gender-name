# About Gender Name

Gender Name was created and maintained by the community, it was an initiative by [Theryston](https://github.com/Theryston), a developer who is still on the project maintaining and improving it daily. This is a contribution to solving problems in an innovative way

## Help Us

The AI models are hosted on Replicate, which means there are costs involved in the process. For every second the model runs we are charged $0.0001, so we don't really need your help to keep our models running: [Buy Me a Coffe](https://www.buymeacoffee.com/theryston)
